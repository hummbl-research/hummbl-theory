## Connection to Base120

Klein's Erlangen Programme redefined geometry from object-centric to operation-centric. Base120 makes the same move for cognition: the 780 models are invariant residues under 6 transformation operators. The 6 operators (P, IN, CO, DE, RE, SY) define the transformation group.
